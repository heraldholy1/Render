Tokenomics & Supply Framework

MYCOIN TOKEN ("MYC")

Overview

The token is currently issued as a Solana SPL precursor token intended to support early ecosystem participation while the project's native blockchain and native coin are under development.

The precursor token will be eligible for a one-to-one upgrade into the native coin once the native chain is live. The precursor token is not designed to coexist permanently with the native coin.

Regulatory Positioning

The token is not registered with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission and is not offered as a regulated investment product.

The project does not make representations regarding profit, returns, or price appreciation. Any market value is independently determined by participants and market activity.

Token Type and Authority

Standard: Solana SPL Token
Mint Authority: Active
Freeze Authority: Disabled
Governance: None at the token level

This structure ensures full user custody, prevents unilateral freezing of user funds, and allows founder-led control during early development.

Supply Summary

Current Minted Supply: 4.68 trillion tokens
Actively circulating and usable

Target Supply Cap: 20 trillion tokens
Soft cap by design
No immutable hard limit

The absence of a hard cap allows the project to respond to demand without artificial constraints.

Supply Expansion Philosophy

Supply expansion may occur in response to demonstrated ecosystem demand, liquidity requirements, or strategic or operational needs.

Additional minting may or may not be tied to a specific predefined use case at the time of minting. Demand itself is considered a valid justification in early-stage networks.

Strategic Reserve ("Piggy Bank")

Structure: Exists as unminted supply | Not held in wallets | Not circulating

Purpose: Liquidity provisioning | Strategic partnerships | Ecosystem expansion | Contingency or stabilization actions

Control: Founder-controlled in early phases | All mint events are on-chain, publicly verifiable, and disclosed through official channels

Pricing and Pre-Market Discovery

Initial reference price is one U.S. dollar per token.

Private transactions may occur at negotiated prices. Pre-market pricing reflects private OTC activity, bilateral agreements, and issuer reference pricing. This is not equivalent to public market price discovery.

Migration to Native Chain

Mechanism: User-initiated one-to-one upgrade through a bridge interface | Solana tokens are burned | Native coins are minted on the new chain

Guarantees: No double supply | Auditable upgrade path | Wallet ownership preserved where technically feasible

Transparency and Disclosure

Authoritative Source: On-chain data is the authoritative source of supply information.

Disclosure Channels: Official website supply page | Public supply log | Official announcements via email and community channels

Best-effort notification will be provided for material supply events.

Governance

There is no token-level governance during early phases. Governance may exist at the application, project, or ecosystem initiative level.

Risk Disclosure

Supply expansion risk | Founder-led control risk | Migration execution risk | Regulatory uncertainty

Participation should be evaluated based on alignment with the project and ecosystem.
